Series

Game Of Thrones

In a fantastic world and in a medieval context, several families, related to the nobility, dispute the power to dominate the fictional territory of Westeros (Westeros) and take control of the Seven Kingdoms from the Iron Throne, where the king exercises power.

Avatar: The Last Airbender

The series focuses on the journey of 12-year-old Aang, the current Avatar and last survivor of his nation of him, the Air Nomads, along with his friends Sokka, Katara, and later Toph, as they strive to set an end to the Fire Nation's war against the other nations of the world.

The Legend of Korra

The series is set 70 years after the final events of the original series and follows the Avatar Korra, a young girl from the Southern Water Tribe and the reincarnation of Avatar Aang, while traveling to the metropolis of the Republic City to learn airbending and take on a revolutionary group of anti-benders, the "egalitarians".

Mr. Robot

Mr. Robot centers on a young programmer, Elliot, who suffers from a debilitating antisocial disorder and he decides that he can only connect with people through hacking.
